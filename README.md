## 📐 Methodology & Composite Score Calculation

Each idea is evaluated by a dual automated and terrain analysis, producing a global score out of 100 points:

$$\text{Composite Score} = (\text{VC Score} \times 0.5) + (\text{Terrain Score} \times 0.5)$$

- **VC Score (50%):** Y Combinator & Peter Thiel style analysis (Contrarian thesis, Moat, Global scalability, Unit Economics).
- **Terrain Score (50%):** Customer and market reality ("Hair on Fire" urgency, Immunity to native LLM features, Adoption friction, Monetization clarity).
